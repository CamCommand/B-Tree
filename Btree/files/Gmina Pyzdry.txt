   <p><b>Gmina Pyzdry</b> is an urban-rural <a href="/wiki/Gmina" title="Gmina">gmina</a> (administrative district) in <a href="/wiki/Wrze%C5%9Bnia_County" title="Września County">Września County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Greater_Poland_Voivodeship" title="Greater Poland Voivodeship">Greater Poland Voivodeship</a>, in west-central <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Poland</a>. Its seat is the town of <a href="/wiki/Pyzdry" title="Pyzdry">Pyzdry</a>, which lies approximately 20 kilometres (12&nbsp;mi) south of <a href="/wiki/Wrze%C5%9Bnia" title="Września">Września</a> and 59&nbsp;km (37&nbsp;mi) south-east of the regional capital <a href="/wiki/Pozna%C5%84" title="Poznań">Poznań</a>. </p>
   <p>The gmina covers an area of 137.9 square kilometres (53.2&nbsp;sq&nbsp;mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 7,217 (out of which the population of Pyzdry amounts to 3,188, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 4,029). </p>
   <p>The gmina contains part of the <a href="/wiki/Protected_area" title="Protected area">protected area</a> called <a href="/wiki/Warta_Landscape_Park" title="Warta Landscape Park">Warta Landscape Park</a>. </p>
