   <p><i><b>Blesok | Shine</b></i> is the first <a href="/wiki/Macedonian_Language" class="mw-redirect" title="Macedonian Language">Macedonian</a> on-line arts and cultural magazine, published bimonthly, in both <a href="/wiki/Macedonian_language" title="Macedonian language">Macedonian</a> and <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Its founder and editor-in-chief is the poet <a href="/w/index.php?title=Igor_Isakovski&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Igor Isakovski (page does not exist)">Igor Isakovski</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </p>
   <p>"Blesok", officially titled "Blesok – literature &amp; other arts" has been published by the Cultural Institution Blesok since 12 March 1998.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Besides the webzine, the publisher also has significant production of e-books (on-line and on CD-ROMs), and in hardcopy. </p>
