   <p>The <b>Distinguished Service Star</b><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> is the third highest military award of the <a href="/wiki/Armed_Forces_of_the_Philippines" title="Armed Forces of the Philippines">Armed Forces of the Philippines</a>. <sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> It is awarded for eminently meritorious and valuable service rendered while holding a position of great responsibility. <sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> </p>

   <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description_of_the_award">Description of the award</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Distinguished_Service_Star&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description of the award">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
   <p>The medal is worn around the neck, suspended from a ribbon of dark blue, with a red vertical stripe in the center. The medal, as the name would indicate, is in the form of a five pointed star. The star contains a central disc displaying the <a href="/wiki/Coat_of_arms_of_the_Philippines" title="Coat of arms of the Philippines">Coat of arms of the Philippines</a>. Surmounting the seal in an arc is a golden scroll engraved worth "Merit". The star is attached to its ribbon by a wreath of twenty-six dark green leaves and ten white buds. On the reverse of the medal is engraved "For Distinguished Service."<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> </p>
