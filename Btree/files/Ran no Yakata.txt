   <p><b>Ran no Yakata</b> (ランの館) is a public <a href="/wiki/Orchid" class="mw-redirect" title="Orchid">orchid</a> garden in <a href="/wiki/Nagoya" title="Nagoya">Nagoya</a>, central Japan. </p>
   <p>Open in May, 1998. The gardens have more than 250 different species of orchids, displayed in a large glasshouse. The common and most rare orchids are exhibited here. The gardens also host horticultural events. A decrease of visitors, it has been closed by March 31, 2014. The renewed, and scheduled to open in September 26, 2014 as <b>Hisaya-ōdōri gardens</b>（久屋大通庭園）. </p>
   <p>Access by public transport is <a href="/wiki/Yabach%C5%8D_Station" title="Yabachō Station">Yabachō Station</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Meij%C5%8D_Line" title="Meijō Line">Meijō Line</a>. </p>
