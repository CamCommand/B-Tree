   <p><b>Kollum</b> (<a href="/wiki/Dutch_Low_Saxon" title="Dutch Low Saxon">Low Saxon</a>: <i>Kölm</i>) is a village in <a href="/wiki/Noardeast-Frysl%C3%A2n" title="Noardeast-Fryslân">Noardeast-Fryslân</a> municipality in the province <a href="/wiki/Friesland" title="Friesland">Friesland</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a>. It had a population of around 5529 in January 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> There is a restored windmill, <a href="/wiki/Tochmaland,_Kollum" title="Tochmaland, Kollum"><i>Tochmaland</i></a> in the village. </p>

   <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kollum&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
   <p>It was the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Kollum" title="Battle of Kollum">site of a battle</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Eighty_years_war" class="mw-redirect" title="Eighty years war">Eighty years war</a> in 1581. Before 2019, the village was part of the <a href="/wiki/Kollumerland_en_Nieuwkruisland" title="Kollumerland en Nieuwkruisland">Kollumerland en Nieuwkruisland</a> municipality. </p> 
   <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Marianne_Vaatstra_murder">Marianne Vaatstra murder</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kollum&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Marianne Vaatstra murder">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
   <p>The village made national headlines after the <a href="/wiki/Murder_of_Marianne_Vaatstra" title="Murder of Marianne Vaatstra">murder of Marianne Vaatstra</a> and subsequent riots which took place in the area in 1999. The case was only solved 13 years after the murder when the perpetrator was arrested after a DNA match.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </p>
