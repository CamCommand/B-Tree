   <p><b>Pontarlier Airport</b> (<a href="/wiki/ICAO_airport_code" title="ICAO airport code">ICAO</a>: <b>LFSP</b>) is a municipal <a href="/wiki/Airport" title="Airport">airport</a> located near Pontarlier in the department of <a href="/wiki/Doubs" title="Doubs">Doubs</a>, region of <a href="/wiki/Franche-Comt%C3%A9" title="Franche-Comté">Franche-Comté</a>, <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. It is managed by the town of <a href="/wiki/Pontarlier" title="Pontarlier">Pontarlier</a>, and is open to the public<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>. </p>
