   <p><b>Brett Gillard</b> is an Australian former <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league" title="Rugby league">rugby league</a> footballer who played as a <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#The_back_row" title="Rugby league positions">back-row forward</a> in the 1980s and 1990s. </p>
   <p>He played in 40 matches for <a href="/wiki/Sydney_Roosters" title="Sydney Roosters">Eastern Suburbs</a> in the years 1989-92, <a href="/wiki/Penrith_Panthers" title="Penrith Panthers">Penrith Panthers</a> 1993&amp; 94, Gold Coast Seagulls later renamed the Gold Coast "Chargers" 1995 &amp; 96 and a season with the <a href="/wiki/South_Sydney_Rabbitohs" title="South Sydney Rabbitohs">South Sydney</a> club in 1997.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> </p>
   <p>Gillard in currently associated with one of Eastern Suburbs junior clubs', the <a href="/wiki/Clovelly_Crocodiles" title="Clovelly Crocodiles">Clovelly Crocodiles</a>, in junior development. </p>
