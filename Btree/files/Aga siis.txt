   <p>"<b>Aga siis</b>" (English: <span lang="en">"But Then"</span>) is a song performed by <a href="/wiki/Estonia" title="Estonia">Estonian</a> singer <a href="/wiki/J%C3%BCri_Pootsmann" title="Jüri Pootsmann">Jüri Pootsmann</a>. The song was released as a digital download on 13 November 2015 through <a href="/wiki/Universal_Music_Group" title="Universal Music Group">Universal Music Group</a> as the second single from his self-titled extended play. The song peaked at number 1 on the <a href="/wiki/Radio_Uuno" class="mw-redirect" title="Radio Uuno">Estonian Airplay Chart</a>. </p>
