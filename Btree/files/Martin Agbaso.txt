   <p><b>Chief Martin Agbaso</b> (born 1959) is a <a href="/wiki/Nigeria" title="Nigeria">Nigerian</a> economist, politician and was a candidate for <a href="/wiki/Governor_of_Imo_State" class="mw-redirect" title="Governor of Imo State">Governor of Imo State</a>. Agbaso is the former Imo State Senator for Owerri, a special assistant on Ecology to the President of the Federal Republic of Nigeria and is married to Robin Agbaso, an American. The Agbasos have three children. </p>

   <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Formative_Period">Formative Period</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Martin_Agbaso&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Formative Period">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
   <p>Chief Martin Agbaso was born on April 5, 1959 in <a href="/wiki/Emekuku" title="Emekuku">Emekuku</a>, <a href="/wiki/Owerri_North" title="Owerri North">Owerri North</a> Local Government Area of <a href="/wiki/Imo_State" title="Imo State">Imo State</a>. The first son of Chief Nathan and Mrs. Anna Agbaso. </p> 
   <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Martin_Agbaso&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
   <p>Young Martin began his early education at Our Lady's Primary School, Emekuku (now Chief Obi Memorial Primary School), from which he graduated with the First School Leaving Certificate in 1971. </p> 
   <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Family_Life">Family Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Martin_Agbaso&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Family Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
   <p>He married his 19 year old sweetheart, Robin, in New York at the age of 23. They have three children. </p>
