   <p><b>Cletus Clark</b> (born January 20, 1962) is a retired male <a href="/wiki/110_metres_hurdles" title="110 metres hurdles">hurdler</a> from the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>, best known for winning the gold medal in the men's 110 metres hurdles at the <a href="/wiki/1991_Pan_American_Games" title="1991 Pan American Games">1991 Pan American Games</a>. </p>
