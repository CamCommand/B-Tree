   <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Location">Location</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bankapasi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Location">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
   <p>Bankapasi is located at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Bankapasi&amp;params=23.5741_N_88.0473_E_"><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">23°34′27″N</span> <span class="longitude">88°02′50″E</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">23.5741°N 88.0473°E</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">23.5741; 88.0473</span></span></span></a></span>. </p> 
   <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Urbanisation">Urbanisation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bankapasi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Urbanisation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
   <p>88.44% of the population of Katwa subdivision live in the rural areas. Only 11.56% of the population live in the urban areas.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>The map alongside presents some of the notable locations in the subdivision. All places marked in the map are linked in the larger full screen map. </p> 
   <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bankapasi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
   <p>As per the <a href="/wiki/2011_Census_of_India" title="2011 Census of India">2011 Census of India</a>, Bankapasi had a total population of 6,250 of which 3,160 (51%) were males and 3,090 (49%) were females. Population below 6 years was 710. The total number of literates in Bankapasi was 3,770 (68.05% of the population over 6 years).<sup id="cite_ref-census2011_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-census2011-2">[2]</a></sup> </p> 
   <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Economy">Economy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bankapasi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Economy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
   <p>Bankapasi, Ketugram, Palita, Mohanpur, Kamarpara and Parhat are important centres of <a href="/wiki/Sholapith" title="Sholapith">sholapith</a> craft.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> </p>
   <p>In a report by the Micro, Small and Medium Industries Development Institute, it says that the degree of excellence of products like, <a href="/wiki/Sholapith" title="Sholapith">sola</a> craft of Bankapasi and <a href="/wiki/Dhokra" title="Dhokra">dhokra</a> of <a href="/wiki/Dwariapur,_Bardhaman" title="Dwariapur, Bardhaman">Dwariapur</a> is now acceptable to European markets.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> </p> 
   <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transport">Transport</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bankapasi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Transport">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
   <p>Bankapasi is on <a href="/wiki/State_Highway_14_(West_Bengal)" title="State Highway 14 (West Bengal)">State Highway 14</a> {Bardhaman-Katwa Road).<sup id="cite_ref-google_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-google-5">[5]</a></sup> </p>
   <p>There is a station at Bankapasi on the <a href="/wiki/Bardhaman-Katwa_line" class="mw-redirect" title="Bardhaman-Katwa line">Bardhaman-Katwa line</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> </p>
