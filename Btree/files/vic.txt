   <p>The Very Reverend <b>Victor Gilbert Benjamin Griffin</b> (Dean Griffin),<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> (24 May 1924 – 11 January 2017) was a <a href="/wiki/Church_of_Ireland" title="Church of Ireland">Church of Ireland</a> (<a href="/wiki/Anglican" class="mw-redirect" title="Anglican">Anglican</a>) <a href="/wiki/Priest" title="Priest">priest</a>, <a href="/wiki/Theologian" class="mw-redirect" title="Theologian">theologian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Author" title="Author">author</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </p>
   <p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Carnew" title="Carnew">Carnew</a>, <a href="/wiki/County_Wicklow" title="County Wicklow">County Wicklow</a>, Griffin was educated at <a href="/wiki/Kilkenny_College" title="Kilkenny College">Kilkenny College</a> and <a href="/wiki/Trinity_College,_Dublin" class="mw-redirect" title="Trinity College, Dublin">Trinity College, Dublin</a>, where he was elected a scholar and awarded the Luce, Bernard, Wray and Macren prizes for philosophy and metaphysical studies. He was <a href="/wiki/Ordained" class="mw-redirect" title="Ordained">ordained</a> in 1948.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> He held <a href="/wiki/Curate" title="Curate">curacies</a> at St Augustine's in <a href="/wiki/Derry" title="Derry">Derry</a> then at Christ Church, <a href="/wiki/Derry" title="Derry">in the same city</a>. He became <a href="/wiki/Rector_(ecclesiastical)" title="Rector (ecclesiastical)">Rector</a> of Christ Church in 1957, serving until 1969. He was married to Daphne who died from MS on 16 January 1998. He has twin sons Timothy ( died d 5 June 2012) and Kevin. They both married and now he has 5 grandchildren. He was also <a href="/wiki/Prebendary" title="Prebendary">Prebendary</a> of <a href="/wiki/Howth" title="Howth">Howth</a> in <a href="/wiki/St._Patrick%27s_Cathedral,_Dublin" class="mw-redirect" title="St. Patrick's Cathedral, Dublin">St. Patrick's Cathedral, Dublin</a> (1962-1968) then <a href="/wiki/Dean_(religion)" class="mw-redirect" title="Dean (religion)">Dean</a> from 1969 until 1991.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> </p>

   <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Victor_Griffin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
   <p>He was the author of several books including: </p> 
   <ul>
    <li>Anglican and Irish: What We Believe (1976)</li>
